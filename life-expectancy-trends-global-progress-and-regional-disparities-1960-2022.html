Life Expectancy Trends: Global Progress and Regional Disparities (1960–2022)

Global life expectancy has significantly improved from 1960 to 2022, yet disparities persist across regions. While countries like China have made significant strides, regions such as Sub-Saharan Africa have faced health crises that slowed progress. South Africa, in particular, shows the impact of the HIV/AIDS epidemic on life expectancy, contrasting with the steadier improvements seen in the United States.
